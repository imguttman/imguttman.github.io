Pair Programming 101

By: Isaac Guttman and Peter Brown

What is pair programming?

It is a joining a group of people together to program and solve solutions. By seperateing into distinct roles of Driver and Navigator. You are sharing a computer.

Why is pair programming important to DBC

Pair programming limits errors, a second set of eyes cleans up the process, it expands the number of potential solutions by taking in a different perspective. It is what DBC is all about.

Compare Driver and Navigator

DRIVER: Someone who litterally writes out the code. NAVIGATOR: Directs the driver on a certain part of the code, and lays out the steps to solve the problems.
